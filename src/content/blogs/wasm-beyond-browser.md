## The Serverless Dream
We've been promised instant-start serverless functions for years. Containers are too heavy. V8 isolates are tightly bound to JavaScript. WebAssembly, paired with the WASI preview 2 standard, finally delivers a truly language-agnostic, secure, and fast sandbox.
